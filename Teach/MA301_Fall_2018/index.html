HOMEWORK ASSIGNMENTS (read the syllabus as well)

Homework will be due at the beginning of the class on Wednesdays. Late homework will not be accepted.

A grade will be assigned based on completeness and accuracy (for a subset of the assigned problems).
A major component of the course is clear mathematical communication, so explanations should be thorough and precise. Homework will typically cover 2 or 3 lessons taught during the previous week. All pages should be stapled together. Write your full name in capital letters on the first page.
